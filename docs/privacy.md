# Pose4Me Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** July 21, 2026

Pose4Me is built by Brown Code LLC. Our privacy policy is short because we collect nothing.

## Data collection

Pose4Me collects **no data**. There are no accounts, no analytics, no advertising identifiers, no tracking, and no server — the app has no backend at all.

## Camera

Pose4Me uses your front camera solely to guide your stretches. Pose estimation runs **entirely on your device**: camera frames are processed locally in real time and are never recorded, stored, or transmitted anywhere. When a stretch session ends, the camera turns off.

## Your stretch history

Your session history, streaks, and settings are stored locally on your device (and in your device's shared app-group storage so the widget and Apple Watch app can display them). This data never leaves your devices. Deleting the app deletes the data.

## Notifications

Stretch reminders are local notifications scheduled on your device. They do not involve any server.

## Purchases

Optional tips ("Buy me a coffee") are processed by Apple through the App Store. We never see your payment details. Purchases are subject to Apple's own privacy policy.

## Children

Pose4Me does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children, because it collects no information at all.

## Changes

If a future version of Pose4Me ever changes how data is handled, this policy will be updated and the change will be described in the App Store release notes before it takes effect.
